Description NPPP-1 Reference Material (RM) is a dispersion of nanoscale polypropylene (PP) in water. It is intended for use in assessing the biologic effects of nanoscale plastics, calibrating procedures, and developing methods for determining the size and charge of nanoscale materials. A single vial contains minimum 5 mL of nanoscale PP dispersion in pH 8 water.
