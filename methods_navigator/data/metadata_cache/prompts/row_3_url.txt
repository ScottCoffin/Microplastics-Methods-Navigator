The workshop format combined presentations, breakout groups, and participant discussions during two days of meetings. Fourteen oral presentations included research on such broad-ranging topics as the oceanography of the North Pacific, the ingestion of microplastics by marine invertebrates, and persistent organic pollutants in marine plastic debris.
